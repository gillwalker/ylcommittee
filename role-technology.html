Committee Role

Technology

Works closely with YL staff to assess current technology and provide recommendations that enhance ministry efficiency.

← Back to Committee Structure

Overview

As Young Life continues to grow and adapt in a rapidly changing digital world, leveraging the right technology is essential to expanding the reach and impact of the ministry. From volunteer coordination and donor communication to event planning and digital outreach, effective technology can streamline operations and free up more time for direct ministry with kids.

Job Description

  • Evaluate current platforms used for communication, scheduling, donor management, and volunteer coordination.
  • Recommend new tools and technologies that better serve the needs of staff and leaders while aligning with Young Life's mission and budget.
  • Assist with research and setup of systems such as CRMs, event platforms, file sharing, or communication tools.
  • Offer guidance or informal training when new tools are introduced to the team.
  • Serve as a go-to resource for basic troubleshooting and ongoing digital strategy advice.

Basic Strategies to Develop

Key strategies to develop:

  • Develop a simple “technology roadmap” that helps guide the area in adopting helpful tools and systems.
  • Identify and document best practices for technology use within the area office.

Qualifications

  • Experience in IT, digital systems, tech strategy, or project management.
  • Strategic thinker with practical problem-solving skills.
  • Comfortable collaborating with staff and explaining technology in an easy-to-understand way.
  • Passion for supporting ministry through behind-the-scenes work.
