Committee Structure

Every Young Life committee should have a structure that supports the local mission. Below are the roles that make up a healthy, functioning committee — click any role to read the full description.

← Back to Onboarding Home

Each local Young Life area has a committee of adults who believe in the mission of Young Life: to introduce adolescents to Jesus Christ and to help them grow in their faith. You'll find adults from all different backgrounds, careers and churches, including lots of moms and dads who care deeply about kids.

Committees oversee finances, foster donor relations, pray faithfully for kids and for Young Life, plan events like fundraisers and community socials, and support the staff and area director. The best and most fulfilling committee work happens outside of the monthly meeting — through these focused subcommittee roles.

🏛️

Committee Chair

Ensures that committee members share in the ownership of the ministry with the local staff, volunteer leaders, local donors and regional director.

→
🙏

Prayer Team

Collects prayer requests from the local area staff and volunteers and prays together on a regular basis.

→
❤️

Leader Care

Ensures that staff and volunteers receive the necessary support to be effective in the area mission to reach kids.

→
📊

Finances

Provides oversight for the area receipts and expenses, directly impacting the ability to reach more kids and tell them about Jesus.

→
🎉

Events

Plans and executes fundraising events (banquet, golf tournament) in cooperation with staff and volunteers to raise financial support for the area.

→
🤝

Resource Development

Engages with local community groups and businesses to develop partnerships and drive forward the area's strategic vision.

→
💰

Donor Care

Ensures the area's donors are regularly thanked and cared for, communicating the vision and mission throughout the year.

→
📣

Marketing & Social Media

Ensures that all significant Young Life activities, stories, and events are publicized in a timely and compelling manner to the community.

→
💻

Technology

Works closely with YL staff to assess current technology and provide recommendations that enhance ministry efficiency.
