What This MOU Covers

This draft MOU is a template for any Young Life area seeking to formalize a partnership with a local church. It is designed to be customized — replace all bracketed placeholders with the names, details, and agreements specific to your area and church partner.

The Church Commits To…

  • Physical ministry resources (meeting/event space)
  • Financial support ($5k–$15k range for camp expenses and programming)
  • Fundraising within the local church network
  • Recruiting volunteer leaders from the congregation
  • A Missional Committee of 5 local Christian adults (4–7 hrs/month)

Young Life Commits To…

  • Background checks for all volunteer leaders
  • Leadership training for all leaders
  • Cultural Intelligence training for church leadership and volunteers
  • Program costs for club and contact work
  • Snow Camp and Summer Camp access
  • Financial support alongside the church missional committee

Key Principles of a Healthy Partnership

Partnerships developed through this MOU model are built around five principles:

  1. Long-term commitment — with annual review of the MOU
  2. Mobilization — of church members into the community (those interested in youth work or mission community)
  3. Alternative funding structure — shared financial responsibility between YL and the church
  4. Fundamental ministry components — outreach and discipleship as the core focus
  5. Collaborative vision — to develop a sustainable outreach ministry together

Missional Committee Roles

The Missional Committee that forms around each school partnership typically consists of 5 Christian adults from the local community who commit to:

  • Praying for ministry at the school
  • Advocating to the church and their network on behalf of ministry (recruitment)
  • 4–7 hours per month, including a monthly meeting
  • Supporting local staff and volunteers
  • Assisting in raising necessary funds
  • Connecting local staff to potential prayer-partners, volunteers, staff, and donors

How to Use This Template

Click Open & Download Template above to open the document in Google Drive, where you can make a copy and edit it for your area. Replace all bracketed fields with your specific information:

  • [Church Name] → Your partner church’s full name
  • [Young Life Area Name] → Your area’s name
  • [School Name] → The school where ministry will be focused
  • [City] → Your community
  • Update the Roles & Responsibilities section to reflect your actual agreed-upon commitments
  • Have both parties sign and date the agreement

Reach out to your Area Director or the Regional Office if you have questions about adapting this template for your specific partnership context.
