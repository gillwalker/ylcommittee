I

New Committee Chair Call To Action

Your first 90 days are organized into three clear phases. Each builds on the last, moving from personal rhythms → relationship → strategy & engagement.

Phase 1

30 Day Plan

  1. 1
    Create a daily personal prayer plan
  2. 2
    Schedule Weekly 1:1 Meetings with Area Director
  3. 3
    Schedule monthly Committee Meetings
  4. 4
    Review Area Cash Flow on Staff Resources website
Phase 2

60 Day Plan

  1. 1
    Meet with each person on the Committee individually
  2. 2
    Develop Matthew 9:38 list for adult (community) support
Phase 3

90 Day Plan

  1. 1
    Attend Regional Zoom Meeting for Committee Chairs
  2. 2
    Develop a strategic plan for the next 6–18 months (with Area Director and other stakeholders)
  3. 3
    Attend at least two Direct Ministry events (club, campaigners, camp, etc.)
