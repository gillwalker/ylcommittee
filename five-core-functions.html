Five Core Functions of Young Life

Every Young Life Area has five functions and essential work that comprise its operational core. Clarity about these functions helps get talented people into the right roles.

← Back to Onboarding Home

Overview

Every Young Life Area has “Five Functions and Essential Work” that comprise its operational core. Local area adult committee members and a team of volunteer leaders participate in all five components alongside the Young Life staff.

The Five Functions

1. Spiritual Development

Following Christ
  • Prayer and Spiritual Disciplines
  • Fellowship
  • Growth and Health
  • Church Relationships

2. Leadership Development

Equipping Leaders, Committee and Staff
  • Key Volunteer Care
  • Recruiting
  • Team Building and Training
  • Supervision
  • Vision Casting

3. Resource Development

Fueling the Ministry
  • Events
  • Major Donor Care
  • Public Relations (Branding)
  • Relationship Centered Engagement

4. Ministry Support

Taking Care of Business
  • Accounting
  • Administration
  • Communication (Internal)
  • Data Management
  • Strategic Plan

5. Direct Ministry

Proclaiming and Discipling
  • Contact Work
  • Club
  • Campaigners
  • Camp
